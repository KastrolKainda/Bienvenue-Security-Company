About Us

Stay safe, be safe and protect what matters most with our well trained Security Officers

At Bienvenue Private Security, we are dedicated to protecting what matters most - your people, property and peace of mind. Our comprehensive security services are tailored to meet the unique needs of each client, ensuring a safe and secure environment at all times. The following are our top five (5) pillars of our company success:

  • We always communicate amongst ourselves and clients.
  • We always coordinate with our internal staff and clients.
  • We always organize and keep clients request, instructions and concerns.
  • We use technology to deliver security services more efficiently and effectively.
  • We always answer our phones and make sure our team listens to your concerns and questions.

Why you should Choose to Work with Us


Experienced Professional - Our security team is comprised of highly trained and experienced personnel who are dedicated to providing the most top-tier one security services in Zambia.

Customized Solution - We tailor our security services to meet the specific needs of our clients, ensuring maximum effectiveness.

Commitment to Excellence - At Bienvenue, we pride ourselves on delivering reliable, efficient and discreet security solution.

Services

Corporate Security

Safeguarding your business premises, employees and assents with professional security personnel and advanced monitoring systems.

Event Security

Expertly managing the security needs of events of all sizes, from private gathering to large public functions, ensuring a safe and orderly environment.

Residential Security

Providing 24/7 protection for homes and communities with personalized security solutions, including access control and patrol services.

Security Consulting

Offering expert assessments and advice to enhance your security strategies, helping you mitigate risks and prevent security breaches.

Personal Protection

Discrete and reliable bodyguard services for high-profile individuals, ensuring their safety and privacy at all times

Private Investigation
